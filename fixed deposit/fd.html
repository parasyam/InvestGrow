How fixed depost works

A Fixed Deposit (FD) is a savings option where you invest a lump sum with a bank for a fixed period at a guaranteed interest rate, earning returns at maturity.

pros

* Guaranteed Returns
* Low risk
* Flexible Tenure

cons

* Lower Returns
* Taxable Interest
* Lock-In Period
