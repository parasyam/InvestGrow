How mutual funds works

Mutual funds pool investors' money to invest in a diversified portfolio of stocks, bonds, or other securities. Managed by professionals, these funds offer returns based on the performance of the underlying assets, providing growth potential and reduced risk through diversification

pros

* Professional management
* Risk reduction
* Diversification of capital

cons

* Market risk
* Platform Fees
* Limited control
